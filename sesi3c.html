FUDGE BROWNIES

These quick and easy homemade brownies! Made with simple ingredients from my Mom kitchen

INGREDIENTS

Ingredient A

  • 230 gr dark compound coklat
  • 85 gr unsalted butter

Ingredient B

  • 85 gr all purpose flour
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 20 gr choco chips

Ingredient C

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 T coffee
  • 1 tsp Vanila

STEPS :

  1. Melted ingredients A.
  2. Beat ingredient C until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add ingredient A to C. Mixed well.
  4. Add ingredient B to C until well combined.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes at 175 degrees Celsius.
